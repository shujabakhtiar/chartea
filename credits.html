Welcome to Chollerton Tearooms, the coziest little haven nestled in the enchanting hamlet of Chollerton, where warmth, charm, and scrumptious delights await you. Located in the picturesque North Tyne Valley, our tearooms offer the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Step through our doors and immerse yourself in a world of delicious aromas, heartfelt hospitality, and a touch of whimsy that will tickle your taste buds and bring a smile to your face.

At Chollerton Tearooms, we pride ourselves on creating a truly welcoming atmosphere that feels like a warm hug from your favorite aunt. Whether you're seeking solace over a steaming cup of tea, a delightful rendezvous with friends, or simply a sweet treat to brighten your day, we've got you covered. Our team of talented pastry wizards has crafted a mouthwatering menu, filled to the brim with delectable homemade cakes, flaky pastries, and scones so light they practically float off the plate.
